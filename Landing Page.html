Features

Built for firms that serve real businesses.

Every feature is designed to remove a specific manual step — not to be flashy. Turn on what you need, roll it across your entire book of business in a day.

Confidence-gated autopilot
Auto-book anything scoring above the threshold you set — 95%, 98%, or human-only. Everything below goes to a review queue with reasoning attached.
Continuous reconciliation

Firm-wide COA templates
Define how categorization works once, apply it across every client — with per-client overrides.

FAQ

Questions we hear every week.

Didn't find what you need? The team replies within a business day.

Do my clients have to switch off QuickBooks or Xero?
No. Ledge is additive — it reads from and writes back to the GL your clients already use. Nothing migrates, nothing breaks.
What happens when the model gets something wrong?
Every entry is reversible with one click and leaves an audit trail. Corrections feed the model back immediately — we retrain on your firm's data nightly, not quarterly.
Is our client data used to train models for other firms?
No. Per-firm data stays in per-firm scope. Only aggregate, anonymized patterns are shared as optional peer signals, and you can opt out.
How long does onboarding take?
Most firms are running on 10 pilot clients within a week, and rolled out to the full book within a month. The migration team handles data import from Dext, Keeper, or your existing rule library.
What about security and compliance?
SOC 2 Type II, bank-grade encryption at rest and in transit, read-only bank connections by default, and granular role-based access. Full security whitepaper available on request.
Will this replace my bookkeepers?
No — it replaces the part of their job they like least. Teams typically redeploy the reclaimed hours into advisory work, which is where the real client value (and firm margin) lives.
